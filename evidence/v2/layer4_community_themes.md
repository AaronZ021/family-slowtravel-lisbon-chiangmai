### 4.3 季节切换信号（What month is the killer?）

#### 里斯本季节
- **🟥 12 月–2 月（冬季湿冷高峰）**：≥ 6 来源 / 4 平台 — 暖气缺失 + 霉菌 + 80% 湿度，所有 expat 一致呼吁选房关键期

  - Verbatim — "Some people wear jumpers, jackets, sometimes gloves indoors" [S-LIS-122]
- **🟧 7 月–8 月（高温 + 旅游高峰）**：≥ 4 来源 — 35°C+ 对幼儿不友好 + 游客拥挤

  - Verbatim — "Avoid August when Lisbon is crowded mainly with tourists since the locals often leave the city for vacation" — Travellers Worldwide [S-LIS-130]
